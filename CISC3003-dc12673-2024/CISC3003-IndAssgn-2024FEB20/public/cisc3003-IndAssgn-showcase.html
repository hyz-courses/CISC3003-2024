My Statement of Purpose

As a third-year Computer Science student, my academic journey is approaching to its finale, yet I shall say that it has been a rich tapestry of learning and discovery. From the fundamentals of C Programming to the complexities of Natural Language Processing, each course has added a unique thread to this tapestry.

My initial foray into the world of programming began with C & Python Programming, where I learned the importance of syntax and structure, fostering the mindset as an individual working with computers. This foundation was further solidified by courses in Object-Oriented Programming & Data Structures, which introduced me to the power of abstraction and the efficiency of working with classes, having well-organized data. Moreover, the benefits of OOP has never been limited to its own range - The ideas of encapsulation, inheritence and polymorphism together formed the fundamental mind model of me when facing numerous & various questions, including Algorithm Designing, Performance Evaluation, Work Simplification, etc.

Other than softwares, I had also been engaged in the analytical part about hardwares. Courses like Numerical Methods empowered me with the principles of how computers deal with numbers, introducing the beauty of taylor series and matrix operations. I've also got to comprehend the floating point number system in this course.

My understanding of the intricate workings of a computer system has been deepened by my coursework in Operating Systems. I shall say I had never been a valid computer science student until I learned this course. By working on processes, threads and locks, I get to understand the computer's behavior I've encountered, so as to raise a valid solution when being faced with an issue.

The limitless potential of the web has always fascinated me, and my course in Web Programming has allowed me to explore this potential. It has not only sharpened my technical skills but also broadened my understanding of the intricate aspects of user experience and interface design.

Looking ahead, I aspire to leverage my diverse academic background to create innovative solutions that can make a significant impact. I am particularly intrigued by the intersection of web development and artificial intelligence. I believe that the amalgamation of these two fields can lead to the creation of more intuitive and user-friendly platforms.

This statement is a testament to my passion for Computer Science and my unwavering dedication to my field. As I continue on this exciting academic journey, I eagerly anticipate the challenges and opportunities that await.
